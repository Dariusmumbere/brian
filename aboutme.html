About me

Munganga Brian is a passionate social activist, youth worker, and community educator. Currently in his final year at Kyambogo University, Brian is dedicated to uplifting his community through various forms of social engagement and education. He combines his academic pursuits with a strong commitment to grassroots activism, aiming to bring about meaningful change for youth and marginalized groups in his community.

Brian is committed to enhancing education at the grassroots level. He has led several community outreach programs, where he provides education on topics like civic engagement, health awareness, and personal development

As a youth worker, Brian has consistently worked with young people to provide mentorship, guidance, and opportunities for growth. He runs workshops, leadership programs, and community initiatives designed to equip the youth with skills and knowledge to improve their lives and contribute positively to society.
